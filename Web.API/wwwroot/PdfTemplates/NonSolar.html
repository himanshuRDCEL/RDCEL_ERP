  What Should You Look For 

  In A Solar Company?

 
 
 

1. Accuracy For Return On Investment Calculations


Any company pitching to you a proposal to install a major piece of equipment which is meant to 

lower your power bills should also be able to give you an  accurate projection of figures that you 

can rely on before making a decision.


2. A Company Who Can Demonstrate Your Solar Energy Will Mostly Be Used On-Site


Using your solar energy is important! It's what dictates a good investment from a mediocre investment. 

If you make a poor choice then the solar system you  choose to install may be too large for your needs 

or might not fit in with your consumption pattern. So a solar company who can demonstrate how a solar 

energy  system will interact with your current use is a company you can trust.


3. A Company With Experience


It almost goes without saying that experience is highly important in any type of business. Nobody wants

to buy from the company who just opened up shop and  hasn't had a wealth of experience. The solar 

company you choose should be able to demonstrate that they have experience installing systems similar

to the one  they propose to sell to you.


4. A Company With Happy Customers


Clearly a solar company who has many happy customers is a safer bet than a company whose customers

are invisible. You would be wise to check third party websites for customer reviews of the company you're 

considering before putting pen to paper and committing to buy a solar system.
